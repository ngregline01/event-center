About Us

At DegreeFuel, we believe that education should be accessible, empowering, and community-driven — no matter where you’re from. Founded to uplift immigrants, international students, and first-generation scholars, DegreeFuel is more than just a platform — it’s a movement. Through inspiring events, mentorship link-ups, scholarship opportunities, and storytelling workshops, we create space for students to thrive, connect, and be seen.Our mission is to fuel the dreams of those navigating new academic and cultural landscapes by providing tools, opportunities, and a strong support network. Whether you're pitching your vision at our Fuel My Future night, finding hands-on experience through volunteer events, or getting matched with a mentor who gets your journey — we are here to back you every step of the way. We don’t just help you earn a degree — we fuel the future behind it.
